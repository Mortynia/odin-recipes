The world's best pasta pasta-salad recipe

Fresh, colorful, and full of flavor â€” pasta salad is a perfect dish for picnics, BBQs, or a light lunch. This recipe combines tender pasta with crunchy vegetables and a tangy dressing for a refreshing meal.

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta (fusilli or penne)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1 cup cucumber (diced)
  • 1/2 cup red onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup black olives (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese (crumbled)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and let cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine pasta, tomatoes, cucumber, onion, olives, and feta.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour dressing over salad and toss to combine.
  5. Chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes before serving for best flavor.
