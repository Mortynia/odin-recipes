The world's best burger recipe

Nothing beats the taste of a freshly made burger — juicy patties, melted cheese, and crisp vegetables all in a soft bun. This recipe will help you make a classic homemade burger that’s just as good, if not better, than your favorite diner’s.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Ketchup, mustard, or preferred sauce

Instructions

  1. Form the ground beef into 4 equal patties. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook patties for 3–4 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  4. In the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty and cover to melt.
  5. Lightly toast the burger buns on the grill or in a pan.
  6. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onion, patty, and sauce.
  7. Serve immediately with fries or salad.
